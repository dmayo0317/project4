Forecasting Cryptocurrency

Using ARIMA to Forecast Four Types of Cryptocurrencies.

In order to create our forecasting, we needed to use a model that could handle different points in time while also providing us future time periods. A great model to handle this was ARIMA, short for Auto-Regressive Integrated Moving Average which explains that a given time series is based on its own past values and creates its own equation to be used to forecast future values.

Along with deciding our time series model we also needed to identify the appropriate ARIMA model for time series data. We needed to begin by identifying the orders for our model equation. By using SARIMAX, we were able to evaluate if the order would fit the model because there are more than one order that is used. SARIMAX stands for Seasonal AutoRegressive Integrated Moving Average with eXogenous regressors.

After modifying our model and adjusting it to our seasonal fit, we were able to produce the results below for Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, and XRP.
