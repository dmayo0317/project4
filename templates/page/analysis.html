Using Tableau For Data Analysis


How do I use this table?

To choose which crypto you’d like to see, click the three lines in the upper left corner. You’ll see a graph with the four cryptos and their symbols. Click the symbol of the crypto you’d like to see then click the white ‘X’ to close the graph. Now you can see the data that you chose!

What does this graph mean?

This graph uses a Japanese candlestick chart and line charts to summarize cryptocurrency data for Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, and XRP from 6 December 2016 - 2021.

  • Japanese candlesticks are a clever way of visualising tick data with ample information that tells us a story about the price movement. The four components of a candlestick are the price for open, high, low, close.
  • Line Charts use points connected by line segments to demonstrate changes in values. The two line charts represent the Market Cap Value (total dollar market value) and Total Volumes of crypto sold.
